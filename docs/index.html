Wenting Zong

Hello! I recently completed my Ph.D. in Bioinformatics at the China National Center for Bioinformation(CNCB), where I focused on multi-omics data analysis and tool development for single-cell epigenome data. Before that, I led bioinformatics efforts in my research team, working extensively on transcriptomics, DNA methylation, ChIP-seq, and single-cell omics.

My work centers on developing computational methods and databases for multi-omics integration. I have designed and implemented several bioinformatics databases and knowledge bases, including single-omics and species-specific multi-omics resources. I also independently developed a specialized tool for single-cell DNA methylation analysis, integrating pipeline construction, algorithm design, data structure optimization, and visualization.

Beyond database and tool development, I have built online analysis platforms for personalized cancer annotation and network interaction analysis, enabling streamlined access to complex biological data.
